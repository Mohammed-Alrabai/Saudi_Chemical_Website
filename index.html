About Us

Saudi Chemical Holding Company

Saudi Chemical Company Ltd is a leading manufacturer of civil and military explosives with three factories in Riyadh, Jeddah, and the Northern Borders Area, along with multiple distribution sites across the Kingdom.
They supply civil and military explosives and services to local and international markets. The company is actively pursuing its strategic objectives to diversify its investments and services.
As part of this strategy, Saudi Chemical Company has acquired and established several specialized companies in the distribution and manufacturing of pharmaceuticals, medical products, and equipment.
